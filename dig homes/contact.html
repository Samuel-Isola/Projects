FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What documents do I need to purchase property?

You'll need a valid ID (Driver's License, International Passport, or National ID) and proof of address. For corporate purchases, we require company registration documents and authorization letters.

How long does the property documentation process take?

The documentation process typically takes 4-6 weeks from payment completion. We ensure all legal documents including survey plans, deed of assignment, and governor's consent are properly processed.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans ranging from 3 to 12 months depending on the property. A 30-50% down payment is typically required, with the balance spread over the agreed period.

Can I visit the properties before purchasing?

Absolutely! We encourage site visits. You can schedule a site visit through our contact form or by calling us directly. Our team will arrange transportation and provide a guided tour.

What happens after I purchase land?

After purchase, we handle all fencing, perimeter survey, and documentation. You'll receive a comprehensive property pack including all legal documents. Our team remains available for any post-purchase support.
