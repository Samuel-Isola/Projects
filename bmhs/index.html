Welcome to BMHS

Baptist Model High School Ilorin is a prestigious mission school located in Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria. Established with a vision to provide quality education grounded in Christian principles, our institution has been shaping young minds for decades.

We are committed to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education that prepares students for future challenges while instilling strong moral values.
