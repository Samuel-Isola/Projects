Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to meet the standards set by the Nigerian Educational Research and Development Council (NERDC) and prepares students for both the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) and the National Examination Council (NECO).

We balance academic rigor with co-curricular activities to ensure holistic development. Our students are encouraged to participate in sports, clubs, and community service to develop leadership and social skills.

With a low student-teacher ratio, we ensure personalized attention and support for every student's academic journey.
