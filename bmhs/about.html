About Our School

Excellence in Education Since Our Foundation

Our History

Baptist Model High School Ilorin was established with a vision to provide quality education grounded in Christian principles. For decades, our institution has been at the forefront of academic excellence in Kwara State.

Founded by the Baptist Mission, our school has maintained a tradition of excellence in both academic and character formation. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the modern world while upholding strong moral values.

Over the years, BMHS has produced outstanding alumni who have excelled in various fields including medicine, law, engineering, education, and public service.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops students academically, spiritually, and morally, preparing them for responsible citizenship and leadership.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and Christian values.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Discipline, Service, and Faith - the core principles that guide our educational approach.
